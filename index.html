ABOUT

The stray animals are one of the most abandoned living beings which face a great deal of negligence.Notwithstanding the love which the pets get, these four-legged creatures are deprived of the essential commodities like food,shelter and treatment.Our vision is a world, in which animals are appreciated and treated with respect according to their needs.

With the current scenario of Covid-19 , even fewer volunteers and resources are available to look after them and this calls for us to contribute now more than ever.These furry four-legged creatures are suffering as the lockdown has left them wandering on the streets for food as they are reliant on the scraps given by the public such as passersby, restaurants, and so on.

This is to all those animal lovers to come forward and unite for a cause that will ensure the healthy living conditions for these innocent and loving creatures.This is to take a small step towards humanity.


OUR WORK IS MADE POSSIBLE THROUGH DONATIONS. PLEASE HELP US MAKE A CHANGE AND IMPROVE THE LIVES OF THOUSANDS OF ANIMALS!

HOW YOUR DONATIONS HELP

Provision of Food

We create standardized food kits to support each animal with food for a month.Our partner non-profit organisations and on-ground volunteers will ensure responsible distribution of these kits. 100% of the funds will be used in procuring and distributing these kits to partner organizations. We will disclose the use of proceeds in a financial transparency report sent to all contributors to the campaign.

Treatment of ailing animals

Road traffic accidents, parvo virus infection, severe maggot wounds, tick fever are just some of the problems that stray animals face while living on the streets. Cases that require more intensive treatment and round the clock care are rescued and transferred to a veterinary hospital.We strive in expanding animal birth control, rabies vaccination, and veterinary services to keep animals and populations safe

Provision of shelter to homeless animals

These animals are brought to animal shelters for their intensive care.The construction and maintenance of these shelter homes are brought about by your kind donations. We believe in bolstering the animal foster and adoption system to match stray animals with loving homes
